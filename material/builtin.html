Overview¶

The library of available material models in Matmodlab is not intended to provide comprehensive coverage of material behaviors. Rather, it provides enough coverage to

  • test Matmodlab capabilities; and
  • demonstrate the material model interface to be adopted by user materials.

Available Materials¶

  • Linear Elastic Material
  • Perfectly Plastic Material
  • Transversely Isotropic Elastic Material
  • Mooney-Rivlin Hyperelastic Material
